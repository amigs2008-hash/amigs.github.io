Description: The Stand Alone Senior High School (SHS) within Taguig Integrated School is a publicly funded educational institution specifically designed for senior high school students, encompassing Grades 11 and 12. This particular SHS operates independently, situated on a distinct campus separate from the junior high school section of Taguig Integrated School. Its autonomy is further emphasized by having its own dedicated School Head, who leads the institution independently of the leadership overseeing the junior high section.
